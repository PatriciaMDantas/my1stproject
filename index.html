Ceropegia Wooddii

Love will definitely be in the air with this charming, trailing semi-succulent vine.
Ceropegia Woodii has small heart shaped leaves, with a delightful green lace pattern, that grow in opposite pairs along a trailing vine. The pinkish-purple undersides of the leaves and stems add a romantic hue. Also known as String of Hearts, it is a perfect plant for hanging baskets. Is extremely easy to look after and very tolerant of neglect.
